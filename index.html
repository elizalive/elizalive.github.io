This animation started with a fascinating interview I conducted with researcher Stephanie Coontz about the complicated story of marriage in human history. I storyboarded, animated, and produced this explainer with design help from artist Annie Brown. DecodeDC used the animation during a live event in partnership with WCPO Cincinnati around the changing face of marriage in the US.

When Rand Paul threw his hat in the Presidential ring, writer Abby Johnston and I teamed up to create this unconventional comparison. We collaborated on the writing and I created the illustration for DecodeDC.

When President Obama announced his plan to make community college free, I pitched, reported, voiced and produced this episode of DecodeDC to give listeners a sense of what free community college looked like in a place that had tried it before-- Tennessee. Turns out, Obama's plan may have been missing some key ingredients.

For election night 2014, I designed and coded this web app to track the results of the most critical U.S. Senate races. The app was fed by a Google Spreadsheet that I updated on election night, and was published on the DecodeDC election night live blog, as well as utlized by on-air correspondants during Scripps' election night coverage.
